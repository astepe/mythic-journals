<h1 class="page-title">NPCs</h1>
<p class="page-description">The people the company has encountered in their travels through Cloverfell.</p>

## Encountered

| NPC | Role | Status |
|-----|------|--------|
| **Wrundil** | Lady of the Pond | Dead. Killed by Xanthe and Tuck Tuck. Body decomposed into plant matter. |
| **Roose Dorka** | Fisherman, devoted to Wrundil | Alive. Warned the company not to trust Wrundil. |
| **Gowere & Arcturus** | Mercenaries at Dome | Alive. Tuck Tuck made an advance on Gowere; Arcturus took offense. |
| **Berav** | Vassal Mercenary, ruler of Dome | Alive. Hospitable host. Spoke fondly of Christov's bread. |
| **Ector** | The Weeping Seer | Alive. Gave conflicting advice about Wrundil. Sobs constantly. |
| **Pryer** | Guard at Pristine's tower | Alive. Left to search for the Cudgel. Got swarmed by bees. |
| **Duchess Pristine** | Dreamer, niece of Sir Nieolas | Alive. Sleeps 15 hours a day. Her dreams may be prophetic. |
| **Torrin** | Pryer's brother, aspiring knight | Not yet encountered. A Don Quixote figure wandering the realm. |
| **Darian** | Blacksmith | Alive. Spotted at Haven with a cart full of ore. Xanthe's former enslaver. |
