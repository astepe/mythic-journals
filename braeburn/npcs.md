<h1 class="page-title">NPCs</h1>
<p class="page-description">The people the company has encountered in their travels through Braeburn.</p>

## Encountered

| Name | Role | Status |
|------|------|--------|
| Ivan the Malt Knight | Ruler of Sunken Spire. Painted the spire. Lent Igor his boat, the *Althea*. Cousin Pritchard built it when they were children. | Alive — friendly to Igor. |
| Lionel Thaddeus | Turnip farmer near Sunken Spire. Fled the fog; travelled with Igor in chapters 1–2. Received the grieving horse from the mud. Returned home shaken. | Alive — back home, apparently a mess. |
| Jago Ragge | Husband of Evaine; father of Tresara. Lay ill in the hut with his wife; barely responsive. | Alive — condition unknown. |
| Evaine Ragge | Wife of Jago; mother of Tresara. Desperate for help when Igor broke down the door. | Alive — condition unknown. |
| Tresara Ragge | Daughter of Jago and Evaine. Had gone to Sunken Spire to find medicines for her parents. | Not yet found by the company. |
| Goyon the Apple Splicer | Created the invasive strain of fuzzy, colourful apples spreading across the realm. | Unknown. |
| Rekha | Previous knight and ruler of Sunken Spire. Dug the pit in the town square. Buried in the graveyard outside town. | Dead. |
| Gerath | Apothecary in Sunken Spire. Gave Igor a potion and asked for information about the choking fog; hoped to name the disease. | Alive. |
| Melisande | The Jewelled Seer. Dwells beneath a singing mushroom on an island upriver. Hidden under layers of jewelled silk. | Alive — gave Igor a task. |
| Bedivere | Melisande's pet fox. Wanders the realm, witnessing death. Always smiling. | Alive — last seen in a tree near Davith, then vanished. |
| Davith | A joyous man with a bow and pointy cap. Had thought the encroaching fog that choked people in their sleep was a hoax. Obsessed with tracking Bedivere the fox. Collapsed from a panic episode. Told Igor what Lionel had said about his encounters with the salt knight. | Alive — recovering, headed home with his son. |
| Gareth | Davith's young son. Brave but frightened when his father collapsed. | Alive — with Davith. |
| The Shepherd | An unnamed man found by the river, attacked and killed by wolf crabs. | Dead. |
| Hygo | Igor's squire. Gruff, mid-to-late 40s; former guard at Sunken Spire. Nervous, hypertensive. | Alive — travelling with Igor. |
